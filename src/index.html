We Are
Leo Club of Homagama Central
Homagama Central provides a versatile environment for youth to learn, grow, empower and take an active role in making an impact. All of our Leos; both past and present, have demonstrated outstanding leadership, excellence and integrity in community service and volunteerism making LCHC one of the most reputed clubs within the district. We empower the future generation to engage in activities that promote humanitarianism and nature through volunteerism by creating an oasis of opportunities through effective leadership, collaboration, hard work, all while having fun. At LCHC, you will be part of the journey where we work together with collective minds, joint effort and equal dedication to reach greater heights

"Motivated To Revamp"
In order to commemorate 15 years of dedicated service to our community, we have decided to relaunch our brand with more purpose than ever.
Vision
Be a driven organization that connects people to revamp humanity and nature.
Mission
Empowering people and nature by mobilizing the power of volunteerism and the generosity of donors to act promptly in the face of global problems and emergencies.
